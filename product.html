Meal Made Easy

    • How to prepare


    Mix the buckwheat flour, salt and baking powder in a bowl.
    Add your Alpro Plain with coconut.
    Combine the wet and dry mixture and blend.
    Heat up some coconut on a medium heat, add 3tbsp to the pan.
    Flip over and cook for another 2 minutes.
    Serve topped with Alpro plain with coconut and promegranate seed


    Ingredients

    200ml Aplro plain with Coconut
    250g buckwheat flour
    A pinch salt
    pomegrante seeds and chia seeds
